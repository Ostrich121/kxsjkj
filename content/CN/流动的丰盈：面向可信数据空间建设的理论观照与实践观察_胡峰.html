[ 目的/ 意义] 可信数据空间构建了一种“数据不动价值动”的新型流通范式，对于促进数据合规流通、释放数据要素价值具有战略意义。[ 研究设计/ 方法] [ 研究设计/ 方法] 基于“概念切入点—功能投射点—现实立足点—案例聚焦点”的研究思路，对可信数据空间进行理论与实践相结合的系统分析。理论观照上，从内涵与属性两方面界定可信数据空间，并论述空间建设的应然意义和实然效用。实践观察上，先从区域和领域两个层面，对可信数据空间建设进行全景刻画；在此基础上，聚集欧洲健康数据空间（EHDS）和温州健康数据空间（WHDS）两个案例，从要素、保障、功能三方面对其建设机制进行解构。[ 结论/ 发现] [ 结论/ 发现] 通过共识规则、多方主体、数据资源、关键技术、应用场景的耦合关联，并基于共享、控制、治理、透明和互操作属性，可信数据空间不仅可纾解数据流通困境、应对数据安全风险，也能推动单一数字市场发展、赋能社会数字治理。国内外通过“法治+ 技治”的方式，有序推进多领域的可信数据空间布局与发展。EHDS 和WHDS 通过多元要素的协同参与、制度和技术的双重保障、数据权益调适的主要功能，释放健康数据共同体的价值红利。[ 创 [ 创新/ 价值] 新/ 价值] 在丰富可信数据空间学理研究面向的同时，为可信数据空间高质量发展和赋能数字经济建设提供决策支持。

Abstract: [Purpose/Significance] Trusted data spaces have built a novel circulation paradigm of data characterized by "data remaining

stationary while value flows", which is of strategic significance for promoting the compliant circulation of data and releasing the value of data elements. [Design/Methodology] Based on the research idea of "conceptual entry point-functional projection point-realistic foothold-case focus point", this paper conducts a systematic analysis of trusted data spaces by integrating theory and practice. In terms of theoretical reflection perspective, it defines trusted data spaces from connotation and attribute, and explores the ought-to-be significance and actual utility of constructing such a space. In terms of practical observation perspective, it first provides a panoramic depiction of the construction of trusted data spaces from region and field; on this basis, two cases, the European Health Data Space（EHDS）and the Wenzhou Health Data Space（WHDS）, are focused to deconstruct the construction mechanism from the three aspects of elements, guarantees and functions. [Findings/Conclusion] Through the coupling association of consensus rules, multiple stakeholders, data resources, key technologies, and application scenario, and leveraging the attributes of sharing, control, governance, transparency, and interoperability, trusted data spaces can not only alleviate the difficulties in data circulation and deal with data security risks, but also promote the development of a single digital market while empowering social digital governance. Through the the approach of "rule of law + technology governance", the layout and development of trusted data spaces are being promoted in an orderly manner across multiple fields at home and abroad. EHDS and WHDS release the value dividends of the health data community through multi-element collaboration, dualsystematic and technological safeguards,and by primarily addressing the adjustment of data rights and interests. [Originality/Value] While enriching the theoretical research of trusted data spaces, this study provides decision-making support for the high-quality development of trusted data spaces and empowering the construction of the digital economy.

3 面向可信数据空间建设的理论观照高效的数据流通交易是数字经济发展的关键，但数据供给方往往因担心失去数据控制权而不愿交换、共享数据，可信数据空间便是针对该问题而提出的解决方案。可信数据空间（国外亦称“数据空间”或“共同数据空间”[23]），其核心要义是既能保有数据提供者的数据控制权，又能满足数据使用方的需求，也能为数据要素的流通开放提供可信赖的分布式基础设施。可信数据空间代表了一种遵循关联数据设计原则的数据共存概念，但相较于数据库、数据湖，可信数据空间实现了pay-as-you-go的轻量级数据融合 [24]。而在该概念的最初提出者Franklin等看来，数据空间可以对不同参与者中数据间的关系（或关联）进行建模，基于建模的数据空间可以捕捉参与者之间不同类型的关系/关联，以实现建立在数字信任基础上的跨域和跨界数据互联、互通、互操作 [25]。
